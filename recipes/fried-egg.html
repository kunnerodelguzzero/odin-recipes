Fried Egg

Description

Fried egg, we're in love. Once you master the crispy, olive-oil-fried egg, you're going to want to put one on everything you make. And you should. Here are our favorite ways to use them, from egg-topped grain bowls and hash to messy-but-worth-it sandwiches.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 tablespoons olive olive
  • salt
  • pepper, freshly ground

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a medium nonstick skillet (a 10" skillet is perfect for 2 eggs) over medium-high.
  2. When oil is shimmering, carefully add eggs one at a time, shaking pan gently after each addition.
  3. Cook eggs, shaking pan occasionally to keep them from sticking to each other (a rubber spatula can help nudge them apart if needed), until edges are golden brown, about 2 minutes.
  4. Carefully tilt skillet toward you so that oil pools at front edge of pan.
  5. Spoon hot oil over egg whites, especially anywhere they are still translucent, while avoiding yolks, until whites are set, about 1 minute more.
