Pizza Margherita

Description

No need to head out for dinner when you can whip up this 5-star classic pizza at home.

A classic pizza for all the family. Easy and quick to prepare, guaranteed to be liked by everyone.

Ingredients

  • 100ml Mutti Tomato Passata
  • 2 tbsp chopped basil leaves, plus small leaves to garnish
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 4 bocconcini, sliced 1cm thick
  • 2 tbsp freshly grated parmesan
  • 8 cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Extra virgin olive oil, to drizzle
  • 2 cups (300g) strong (baker's) flour, plus extra to dust

Steps

  1. Sift flour into a large bowl. Stir in yeast , sugar and 1 teaspoon salt
  2. Lightly grease the cleaned bowl with a little oil, then add dough and cover with a tea towel.
  3. Preheat oven to 240Â°C. Knock back the dough by punching it to remove air and divide into 2 balls.
  4. Combine passata or sugo with basil and garlic, then spread over pizza bases leaving a 2cm border.
  5. Drizzle the pizzas with a little more olive oil, garnish with small basil leaves then serve immediately.
