Lasagna

Description

Lasagna with Meat Sauce is a delightful Italian recipe that can be prepared for treating someone special. Loaded with cheese, this lasagna recipe might seem difficult at first with expert cooking skills. However, much to your surprise, the more hard work you will use for making this amazing dish, the more fruitful it will be. This lasagna recipe uses five types of cheese and two types of meat. But, you can replace the pork meat if you do not have it as the dish will turn out well even without it.

If you have to serve this dish in a party, make the preparations well in advance as it is an elaborate dish and might take some time to cook. Handle the final dish very gently or it might disintegrate or crumble. If you do not have all the varieties of cheese mentioned in the recipe, you can also prepare it with the commonly available ones.

Ingredients

  • 1 kilograms ground mutton
  • 2 cup tomato ketchup
  • 2 clove minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon garlic
  • 3 egg
  • 3 tablespoon minced parsley
  • 8 ounce ricotta cheese
  • 9 sliced and slit lasagne sheets
  • 3 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 750 gm pork sausages
  • 180 ml tomato puree
  • 2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 2 pinches oregano
  • 3 cup cottage cheese (paneer)
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesa cheese
  • 50 gm provolone cheese
  1. Cook the pork and mutton Meat
  2. Prepare the meat Sauce
  3. Mix all the cheese with eggs
  4. Make the layering using cheese, lasagne sheet and meat sausages
  5. Bake the lasagna for 50 minutes at 375 degrees Farenheit
